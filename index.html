Services

Professional fiduciary services

Expert, impartial, and compassionate service across all areas of professional fiduciary practice. If you are unsure which service applies to your situation, please reach out. Most families do not know exactly what they need until we talk.

01
Professional Trustee

When a trust needs an independent, professional hand, I step in as your trustee, managing assets, handling court-formatted accountings, communicating with beneficiaries, and ensuring the trust is administered exactly as intended.

Professional trustees bring objectivity that family members sometimes cannot. I follow your instructions without the influence of family dynamics, personal agendas, or conflict of interest.

02
Trust Administration

Trust administration involves managing and distributing trust assets according to the trust document, often after the death of a grantor, or when a beneficiary can no longer self-manage.

I handle the full scope: record keeping, bill payment, asset management, distributions, tax filings, and communication with all parties, so nothing falls through the cracks.

03
Estate Administration

When someone passes away, their estate must be carefully managed and distributed. This process of gathering assets, paying debts and taxes, and transferring property to heirs can be overwhelming for grieving families.

I take on the full administrative burden, working with your attorney and CPA to ensure the estate is settled accurately and in compliance with the law.

04
Conservatorship

When an individual can no longer manage their own financial affairs or personal care due to age, illness, or disability, the court may appoint a conservator. As a professional conservator, I serve in the best interest of the conservatee, managing their finances, coordinating their care, and reporting to the court.

05
Power of Attorney Agent

Under a durable power of attorney, I act as your agent to manage financial or healthcare decisions when you are unable to do so yourself. This is an important planning tool that can help families avoid conservatorship proceedings entirely.

06
Probate Administration

Probate is the court-supervised process of distributing a decedent's assets. When there is no trust, or when assets fall outside a trust, probate is required. I handle all aspects, from filing the initial petition to final distribution, working closely with your probate attorney throughout.

07
Special Needs Trust Administration

A Special Needs Trust allows a person with a disability to receive trust assets without losing eligibility for government benefits like SSI and Medi-Cal.

I provide attentive, individualized SNT administration, working within trust provisions to support the beneficiary's quality of life while protecting their benefit eligibility.

08
Advisor to Family Fiduciary

Sometimes a family member is named as trustee or conservator and simply needs guidance. I serve as a professional advisor, helping family fiduciaries understand their duties, stay compliant, and avoid costly mistakes, without taking over the role entirely.

09
Pet Trust Administration

California law recognizes pet trusts, allowing you to provide for the care of your animals after you are gone. As your pet trustee, I ensure that funds are used for your pet's care as you intended, working with designated caregivers to protect the animals you love.
